People's Republic of Bangladesh

The People's Republic of Bangladesh, commonly known as Bangladesh, is a country in South Asia characterized by its rich cultural heritage, history of resilience, and ongoing development efforts. Here is an in-depth overview of various aspects of the country:


    Geography

  • Location: Situated on the Bay of Bengal, bordered by India on the west, north, and east, and Myanmar on the southeast.
  • Topography: Predominantly low-lying deltaic plains formed by the Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Meghna rivers. The Chittagong Hill Tracts in the southeast provide some hilly terrain.
  • Climate: Tropical monsoon climate with a hot, humid summer, a rainy monsoon season (June to October), and a mild winter (November to February). Prone to flooding, cyclones, and river erosion.
